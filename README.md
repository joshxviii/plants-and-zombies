<p align="center"><font size=6>A Plants vs. Zombies mod for Minecraft.</font></p>



Plants & Zombies is a Plants vs. Zombies themed mod that aims to add mechanics and characters from the game into Minecraft in a totally integrated, survival friendly way. This mod adds over 20 unique mobs, as well as some unique blocks and items.
</p>

<p align="center">
  <img alt="Page Break" src="https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/1b02c40d42345d59d1bbd86422d32ad842158f5e.png" />
</p>

<p><font size=6>Features & Notes:</font></p>
<details>

- Various types of helpful plants can spawn naturally in specific biomes.

- In order to tame and place these plants, Sun is needed.

- Sun is also used to obtain more plant seeds from plants.

- Sun can be aquired from sun producing plants. However, killing plants will also provide a small amount of sun.

- Naturally spawned zombies have a small chance to spawn as a "Leader" Zombie. These zombies have more health and can spawn reinforcements. These zombies will be carrying a flag.

- Special zombies types can spawn naturally under certain conditions.

- Some special zombies can only be encountered during the Zombie Raid event.

- Plants can only be place on vaild ground or in plant pots.

- Plants that are planted in plant pots have increased defense against zombies and other enemies.

</details>
